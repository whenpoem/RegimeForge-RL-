# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making RegimeForge a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive, patient, and courteous.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Respect different levels of experience and different viewpoints.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, personal attacks, doxxing, spam, and deliberate disruption are not allowed.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code.

## Reporting

Use a private reporting channel whenever possible. Do not use public issues for conduct complaints.
